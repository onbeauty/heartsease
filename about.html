About

Heart’s Ease is a quietly curated repository of ideas, images, notes and gatherings. It is a record of becoming — a place to collect what matters, to hold it with care, and to imagine small assemblies of readers and friends in time.

Eventually the site will host events, workshops, and printed editions. For now, it is a slow archive: one post at a time, chosen with attention.
